Support

You can learn more about the Notion Backups' functionality in our Knowledge Base.

If Notion Backups is temporarily inaccessible, check out our status page.

If you have lost access to your Notion account, see the Recover Lost Notion Data guide.

If you have questions about Notion Backups, feel free to reach us at support@notionbackups.com.

We're not affiliated with Notion Labs, Inc. and can't help you with Notion-related issues. Contact the Notion team instead.
