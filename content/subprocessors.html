Subprocessors

Last updated: December 3, 2024

We use third-party software that may process your information under certain circumstances. We ensure all our subprocessors adhere to strict data protection standards in compliance with GDPR and other applicable regulations.

The following is a current list of personal data subprocessors we use. We keep this list updated as our use of subprocessors changes:

  • Hetzner - Cloud services (hosting) provider
  • Stripe - Payment processing for paid accounts, including limited billing information
  • Sentry - Debugging and monitoring application errors
  • Fastmail - Business communication with customers
  • Postmark - Sending transactional emails (e.g., password resets, notifications)
